Wireless boundary and infrared (IR) sensor

Coin cell | AA | Industrial

Third party IR beams and boundary sensors, including pressure pads, can be connected to a Spook wireless dry contact sensor allowing for a solution to any monitoring issue. If the sensor detects movement or pressure OmniWatch sends an alert.

By installing IR beams or pressure pads, any vulnerable area can be monitored with ease.
