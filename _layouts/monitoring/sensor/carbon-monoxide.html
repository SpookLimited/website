Spook wireless CO sensors monitor for CO. Within a small footprint, the MEMS-based device packs a 0–1,000 PPM measurement range & automated data logging.

  • Measurement range of-1000 PPM
  • ± 1 PPM resolution
  • Accurate to ± 2% of reading or 1 PPM
  • Alerts contain CO level in PPM
  • Customisable alerting based on specified CO levels (sensitivity of alerts is adjustable, not just what is deemed dangerous)
  • CO levels are recorded (data logged) in system at every heartbeat
