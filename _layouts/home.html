24/7 real-time monitoring and alerting

Alarm condition

OmniWatch is constantly checking all sensor readings agains client defined sensor levels. Once a sensor reading has exceeded or is less than the required level a real-time alarm is invoked.

Alarm audit

Alarms are tracked to ensure it is safely received by the client. Once staff have acknowledged the alarm it is stored in an alarm log for future reference. This is perfect for incident management reviews and audits. Understanding a sensors trends during alarm activity is hugely helpful in providing evidence led data for any future proofing, load balancing and planned preventative maintenance programmes.

Alarm escalation

Spook's alarm escalation procedure is perhaps the most comprehensive in the industry. Alarms are sent via email and 2-way SMS. If there is no response the alarm escalates to Spook's 24/7 UK based call centre where alternative phone numbers including landlines can be listed to call. This is perfect for when mobile phones are out of signal, in silent mode or simply lost. All activity is posted to the alarm log. An agents recording can be requested to assist the alarm audit procedure.
