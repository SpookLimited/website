Customisable 24/7 monitoring and alerting

Out of line condition

The system is constantly checking all data against client dictated trip values. Be it too high a temperature reading, detecting a door has opened or even power loss to a location, OmniWatch checks all readings in real-time.

Generate an alert

If an out of line condition is discovered the system will generate an alert and log it on the system - gathering all related data to help you better understand what is happening.

Alert staff members

Alerts are communicated to key memebers of staff via email, SMS and phone calls to ensure that everyone that needs to know is informed as quickly as possible.
