Wireless powder detection sensor

Spook wireless powder level sensors have an 8 inch ribbon which detects the level of powder. As the ribbon is a solid-state sensor it can be used for measuring non-corrosive water based liquids too.

There are no moving parts, for instants floats and levers, therefore the sensor is not susceptible to wear and tear when compared to traditional powder and fluid sensors.
