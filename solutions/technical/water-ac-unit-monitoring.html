AC water ingress

When a wall mounted AC unit leaks it typically sprays and spits water from its oscillating louvres; it's at this dangerous time that your IT equipment and power sockets can be compromised by water ingress.

By installing Spook's AC Water Detection Bundle means you will be alerted to these dangerous events as they happen. This gives you valuable thinking and reaction time to address the issue before it escalates to an event that could cause unplanned IT downtime.

Alarms are sent 24/7 via Spook's award winning 3-tier alarm escalation process. Read more here.

Spook's AC water detection bundle

The bundle is a cost effective, easy to install package that includes everything you need in order to receive water ingress alerts.

The bundle includes

  • 1x LoRaWAN® ethernet gateway for LoRaWan IoT wireless sensors
  • 1x Spook wireless water sensor
  • 1x Wall mounting water tray and associated fixings
  • 1x 1 year subscription to Spook's award winning remote monitoring service; OmniWatch.

Spook wireless water device

Spook pre-configures the unit for easy installation. Simply select your preferred sensor type (as shown below), place the sensor in the water tray and stick the device on the wall. Each sensor comes with an array of wall fixings.

  • Up to 10 years of battery life

Spook wireless ethernet gateway

A small high-performance indoor LoRaWAN gateway for a smart IoT network.

Hundreds of LoRaWAN sensors are available to extend monitoring if required once the gateway is installed.
