Wireless dew point sensor

Temperature and humidity are used to calculate the measurement of dew point. By installing a dual temperature and humidity sensor the dew point is automatically calculated and expressed as °C.

The dual sensors monitor the values in a single casing and are made to a high scientific grade.

  • Scientific grade sensor
  • ± 3% accuracy (between 10%-90% RH)
  • ± 1°C accuracy (1.8°F)
