Wireless dry contact sensor

Coin cell | AA | Industrial

Spook wireless dry contact sensors detect when there is contact between two wired end points. They basically form a loop when closed and should the loop be opened an alert is broadcast and vice versa. These sensors can easily be integrated into existing dry contact panels/ports usually present on third party equipment.
