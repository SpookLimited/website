Spook wireless powder level sensors use a solid-state, continuous (multi-level) sensor for measuring levels in powder and non-corrosive water based liquids. The sensor has an 8 inch ribbon which detects the level within a container.

Monitor and track:

  • Container levels
  • Tank levels
  • Powders
  • Non-corrosive fluid levels
  • Sump pit levels
