Secure cloud based IT equipment and infrastructure monitoring

Spook's cloud based remote monitoring service called OmniWatch is chiefly deployed in data centers and IT server rooms. It is generally well known that the majority of business disruption is caused by unplanned IT downtime.

By monitoring the key environmental and power elements of IT rooms housing critical equipment means if there is an alarm, remedial action can take place to normalise matters in order to maximise business uptime.

Whether there is a requirement to monitor wired climate monitoring hardware or 3rd party devices such as intelligent power strips, uninterruptible power supplies, generators, BMS systems and more; OmniWatch provides an enterprise centric monitoring solution.

Disaster recovery

OmniWatch is designed with disaster avoidance in mind rather than disaster recovery (DR). Disaster avoidance is focused on monitoring key areas for early warnings to events that, if left unnoticed could easily escalate to invoking DR plans.

By taking remedial actions at early stages of a problem means organisations can contain a situation and fix it with little or no disruption to its business.
