Wireless installation instructions

Ethernet gateway installation

NO Component
1 Gateway
2 Antenna 862-873MHz
3 Status LEDs
4 Reset push button
5 RJ45 connector (ethernet)
6 USB port
7 Jack connector (for power supply)

Step one

Connect an ethernet cable to the gateway's (5) RJ45 connector (ethernet) and to your network using a router/switch/patching panel.

Step two

Connect the supplied AC power supply into the (7) Jack connector and plug it into a UPS fed power socket.

Step three

Wait for the first 2 LEDs on the (3) Status LEDs to turn green.

Step four

Confirm with Spook that your gateway is online and sending data.

Warning! Do not switch on any sensors until gateway connectivity is confirmed by Spook.

Wireless sensor installation

NO Component
1 Wireless sensor casing
2 Status LED
3 Function button
4 Screw mounting holes
5 Rear information sticker
6 Internal magnetic mounts

Step one

Note the EUI of the selected sensor, located on the (5) Rear information sticker, and your preffered friendly name (e.g. Rack C, Ceiling and AC2) on your included form.

Step two

Position the sensor in the desired location. A sensor can be installed in a variety of ways including:

A. Screw fixtures

The sensor can be attached to a wall or secure structure using screws through the (4) Screw mounting holes.

B. Magnetic casing

The sensor typically contains two (6) Internal magnetic mounts that can be used to attach it to a magnetic surface.

C. 3M adhesive

For mounting a sensor to a non-magnetic surface Spook includes 3M adhesive strips.

D. Portable

A sensor can also be placed freely on top of racks, tables or wherever readings need to be collected.

Step three

A. Check connectivity

Single press the (3) Function button. If the (2) Status light performs a single flash the sensor is on and has sent data to Spook's servers.

B. No activity

If no activity is observed from the (2) Status light perform a 3 second press of the (3) Function button to turn on the sensor. This will result in a single flash of the (2) Status light.

Warning! Holding the (2) Function button for 5 seconds will perform a factory reset and the (3) Status light will flash 20 times. If this occurs please repeat step three again.

Confirm installation with Spook

Step one

Send your completed form to Spook.

Step two

Once installation is complete and confirmed with Spook you are then able to log into OmniWatch and receive a tour and a demo on how to use your new monitoring solution.
