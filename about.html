About Spook

Spook was founded in 2003 and we have been passionate about remote monitoring from day one and our mission statement since our inception is Prevention is better than cure.

According to Oxford University Press Prevention is better than cure is a proverbial saying, early 17th century. A similar idea is found in a Latin saying of the mid 13th century, it is better and more useful to meet a problem in time than to seek a remedy after the damage is done.

We develop our software to monitor third party hardware. We wanted a name that could be associated with being available 24/7 and have an investigative theme so we landed on Spook.

Why call our remote software monitoring service OmniWatch?

Because our software has been designed to be manufacturer and protocol independent. We can monitor any type of equipment or asset and we wanted a name that reflected this.
