Wireless voltage direct current (VDC) meter

Coin cell | AA | Industrial

Spook wireless VDC meters are used for measuring the voltage of third party devices and batteries. There are a range of different sensor voltages available but the most common Spook meter measures voltage up to 5 VDC. The Spook sensor is really just a voltage observer. If the equipment being measured is passive method of providing excitation voltage is required.

DC stands for Direct Current, which means the voltage is constant (as opposed to AC, alternating current, in which the voltage is constantly oscillating between positive and negative polarity).
