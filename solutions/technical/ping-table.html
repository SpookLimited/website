Introduction to Spook ping tables

Spook have designed ping tables to focus on key assets and ensure they are reachable 24/7. Should any be unavailable, alarms are invoked and handled by Spook's 24/7 real-time 3-tier alarm handling process.

Other network tools can prove too complex and often present alarm fatigue whereby alerts are ignored simply because they are monitoring far too many network points or are being sent to too many users.
