Spook wireless water rope sensors detect conductive liquids anywhere along the length of the detection rope by using two wires covered with conducting polymer.

Place this sensor anywhere near areas susceptible to flooding or areas that have pressurised plumbing.

  • Senses immediate presence of water anywhere along rope
  • 10 foot water rope (expandable in 10 foot sections up to 100 feet)
