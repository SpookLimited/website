Spook wireless pressure meters measure pressure. Connect the wireless pressure meter to a pressurised gas, liquid or vapour supply line, it can measure the pressure within the line.

  • Works with gas, liquid or vapour.
  • No external power required.
  • Measures pressure from 0-50 PSIG.
