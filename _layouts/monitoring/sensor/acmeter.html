Spook wireless alternating current (AC) meters measure the root mean square (RMS) current of an AC system using a current transformer (CT) that wraps around the live wire (also refered to as the hot wire) of a power system. The sensor reports minimum RMS current, maximum RMS current, average RMS current, and amp hours.

  • Measures amp hours, maximum RMS current, minimum RMS current, and average RMS current
  • Capable of generating watt-hour or kilowatt-hour readings
  • Data logging for accumulated amp hour readings
  • Can notify based on current levels or changes in current levels
  • Measures current from 0-20 amps
