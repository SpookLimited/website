Any changes to OmniWatch are recorded in the event log. Each change is clearly explained together with date and time stamps and a note of which user applied them. By recording every change as it happens means as OmniWatch evolves with customer led amendments, they can easily be reviewed at a later date.

There is even a handy notification option to let managers know, via email, changes that are made in real-time.
