The importance of dew point monitoring

Dew point is the temperature at which water vapour in the air starts condensing on the equipment. Environments with high relative humidity (RH) and rapid changes in temperature are most likely to experience condensing humidity. Dew point is always expressed as a temperature (Â°C).
