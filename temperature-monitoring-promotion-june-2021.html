Spook Temperature Monitoring Promotion
June 2021

Traditionally our customers see the summer months as a period when their server room air conditioning units (AC) really are at their most stressed. When it's hot outside, AC has to work overtime in order to keep the room temperature down. If an AC unit is going to break then this is the time its likely to do so. The knock on effect is obvious - possible business outages as critical IT equipment fails or has to be switched off due to abnormally high server room temperatures.

Spook customers relax knowing they will be alerted to temperature issues long before they become a problem. This is by way of email, SMS and UK call centre.

Having time to do even the simplest of actions like opening doors and windows or installing temporary air con units or fans can prevent the nightmare of business disruption and a potential protracted and costly recovery.
