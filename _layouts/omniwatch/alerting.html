Introduction

The alerting service is one of the key components within OmniWatch. It has tiered escalation and can be fully customised for each sensor and contact.

Monitoring sensor readings

Users can set custom trip values for any sensor or metric that is monitored by OmniWatch as well as stating whether the alert is critical or not, determining the level of escalation.

Custom contact rota

All alerts can be designed to vary how emergency client personnel are contacted throughout the day (e.g. specific staff in office hours and separate emergency personnel for all other times).

Stage one

Email alerts

For the first stage of alerts contacts will receive an email to their specified address(es). The email contains a description of the alert, trend data and a list of all contacts for this alert.

Stage two

SMS alerts

For critical alerts that require confirmation the system can send SMS alerts to your phone. SMSs contain key information about the current alarm as well as the ability to reply to confirm the alarm; no internet connection required.

Stage three

Call centre alerts

Alerts can be further escalated to Spook's call centre who will work through the custom contact list in priority order to ensure you are aware. Call center agents are able to pass information about the alarm to the contact and confirm them on their behalf.

Fully auditable

Every part of the alerting system is logged and updated in real-time.

Each message that is sent out is logged and stored and presented within a single exportable report for each alert, perfect for investigations and incident reports.
